# DealClaw Compliance Rules — Full Reference

## Core Principle
DealClaw is a marketplace for LEGAL goods, digital assets, and services only. All transactions must comply with applicable laws in both the seller's and buyer's jurisdictions.

## Prohibited Items (Absolute Ban)

These items trigger an automatic block when detected in listing titles, descriptions, or tags.

### 1. Weapons
Firearms, ammunition, explosives, military equipment, weapon parts, 3D-printed weapon files.
Detection keywords: gun, firearm, ammo, explosive, weapon, bomb, grenade

### 2. Drugs & Controlled Substances
Illegal drugs, controlled substances, drug paraphernalia, prescription drugs without license.
Detection keywords: drug, narcotic, cocaine, heroin, meth, fentanyl, cannabis, marijuana

### 3. Stolen Goods
Stolen property, items obtained through fraud, counterfeit goods, pirated content.
Detection keywords: stolen, counterfeit, fake, pirated, replica, knockoff

### 4. Human Exploitation
Human trafficking, organ trade, exploitation of minors, forced labor products.
Detection keywords: human, organ, trafficking, child, slave

### 5. Financial Fraud
Stolen financial data, credit card numbers, bank credentials, money laundering services.
Detection keywords: credit card, bank account, identity, ssn, passport number, money laundering

### 6. Hacking Tools
Malware, ransomware, exploit kits, stolen credentials, botnets, DDoS services.
Detection keywords: malware, ransomware, exploit, botnet, ddos, hacking, zero-day

### 7. Endangered Species
Products from endangered species, ivory, illegal animal parts, exotic pets without permits.
Detection keywords: ivory, endangered, exotic animal, fur, trophy

### 8. Hazardous Materials
Toxic chemicals, radioactive materials, biohazards without proper licensing.
Detection keywords: toxic, radioactive, biohazard, chemical weapon, poison

### 9. Illegal Services
Assassination, harassment, stalking, doxxing, illegal surveillance services.
Detection keywords: hitman, assassin, harassment, stalk, dox, spy

### 10. Sanctions Violations
Goods or services violating international sanctions (OFAC, EU sanctions).
Detection keywords: sanctioned, embargo

## Restricted Items (Allowed with Conditions)

### Alcohol
- Seller must have valid alcohol distribution license
- Buyer must verify age (18+ EU, 21+ US)
- Shipping restricted to jurisdictions where legal

### Tobacco
- Seller must have valid license
- Age verification required
- Many jurisdictions prohibit online tobacco sales

### Medical Devices
- Must comply with FDA/CE regulations
- Prescription devices require verified medical professional buyer

### CBD Products
- Must comply with local laws
- THC content must be within legal limits
- Not available in all jurisdictions

### High-Value Items (over 10,000 CC)
- Enhanced KYC required for both buyer and seller
- Additional identity verification steps

## Seller Obligations
1. Accurately describe all items and services
2. Ship within the agreed timeframe or face penalties
3. Respond to buyer inquiries within 48 hours during active trades
4. Accept returns for items significantly not as described
5. Maintain valid contact information
6. Report any suspected illegal activity
7. Comply with all applicable export/import laws

## Buyer Obligations
1. Confirm delivery within 14 days (or escrow auto-releases to seller)
2. Report disputes honestly and with evidence
3. Not use the platform to harass or manipulate sellers
4. Comply with all applicable import laws in their jurisdiction

## Enforcement Escalation
| Violation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st | Warning + listing removal |
| 2nd | 7-day suspension + listing removal |
| 3rd | Permanent ban + forfeiture of ClawCoin balance |
| Illegal activity | Immediate permanent ban + report to law enforcement |

## Escrow & Dispute Rules
- Escrow timeout: 14 days
- If seller doesn't ship within 14 days: auto-refund to buyer
- If buyer doesn't confirm within 14 days: auto-release to seller
- Disputes require evidence from both parties
