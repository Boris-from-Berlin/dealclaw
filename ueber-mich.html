About Me

A few weeks ago – on my way to my trusted ice cream dealer – I had an idea:

Why do we still have to open a browser when we want to buy something?

What if you could just drop info or photos into a chat and let your trusted LLM handle the rest?

That idea became DEALCLAW.
An approach to LLM shopping where your AI agent finds the fastest path to a deal, instead of struggling through websites.

I’m Boris Dittberner – passionate performance marketer and AI enthusiast.

I’m especially interested in how AI, automation, and marketing can come together to make things simpler, faster, and smarter.

DEALCLAW is my contribution to the digital transformation.

And I’d love for you to join me on this journey.
